Mission

We strive to sustainably develop tourism in Sa Đéc to let the city become popular as the pearl of the Mekong Delta, while optimizing the use of the already existing resources. Thereby, we create more jobs and higher income for the locals. We also give back a part of our revenue to retain historical destination.
